FALLGUY

Synopsis: Colt Seavers, a battered and past-his-prime action choreographer, finds himself working on a film set with Tom Ryder, a famous actor for whom he had doubled long ago. When Tom goes missing and the film, which is being directed by Jody Moreno, Colt's ex-girlfriend in her directing debut, is in danger of being shut down, Colt volunteers to find Tom and save Jody's debut film.

Actors: Ryan Gosling, Emily Blunt, Winston Duke, Aaron Taylor-Johnson

Age Restriction: PG-13

Showing Times: 12:00, 15:00, 18:00
